# ⚡ npds_nos | Premium FiveM Nitrous Oxide & Purge Calibration System

A state-of-the-art, highly optimized, multi-framework Nitrous Oxide (NOS) and Purge Alignment system for FiveM. 
Designed from the ground up to support standard mechanics roleplay and extreme customization,
this resource offers a premium player experience with a near-zero resource footprint.

---

## 🛠️ Feature Highlights

* **🔩 Chassis Welding Mounts**: Supports realistic **Single-Bottle (1-Bottle)** and **Dual-Bottle (2-Bottle)** custom mount installations inside vehicle chassis.
* **🔋 Standard vs. Elite Cylinders**: Regular gas and high-capacity **Elite Cylinders** with slower consumption profiles and cooler engine operating temperatures.
* **💨 Real-Time Purge Alignment**: Interactive graphical tuner allowing mechanics to visually calibrate purge nozzle alignment, offset, angle, and RGB gas colors.
* **📈 Sleek Glassmorphic HUD**: Beautiful, modern UI showing real-time temperature, dual cylinder percentages, active LEDs, and motion warp screen effects.
* **👮 Police Inspections**: Integrated search commands (`/checknos`) allowing police officers to inspect vehicle engine bays for illegal aftermarket modifications.
* **🌐 Multi-Framework & Localization**: Plug-and-play support for **ESX, QB-Core, QBX, or Standalone** servers, fully translated into English (`en`) and Dutch (`nl`).
